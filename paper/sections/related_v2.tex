\section{Related Work}

\paragraph{Test-time compute scaling.}
Chain-of-thought prompting~\cite{wei2022chain} and self-consistency~\cite{wang2022self} improve reasoning by allocating more compute.
Tree-of-thoughts~\cite{yao2024tree} explores multiple reasoning paths.
Our work reveals the limits of this paradigm.

\paragraph{Adaptive compute.}
Early exit methods~\cite{schuster2022confident} and adaptive depth~\cite{elbayad2020depth} adjust compute per sample.
Unlike prior work focusing on efficiency, we study when more compute \emph{hurts}.

\paragraph{Error propagation in reasoning.}
\citet{stechly2024self} study self-correction failures.
\citet{huang2023large} analyze reasoning errors.
We provide the first systematic study of compute-induced degradation.
